Who Am I

I AM AN OPTIMIST

I heard of the word ''optimism''
from the first time from the speech
of my role model professor PLO Lumumba,
a Kenyan lawyer who claims to be an
Afro-optimist. I followed carefully
the speech and I finally found
the description he gave the word
matching, without any uncertainty,
my identity. I have from then, conducted
different researches on different
people whose deeds have affected positively
Africa and the world who in
addition were optimists and finally
found that my identity contains
a crucial character. 
Why am I so,
Africans like Nelson Madiba Mandela, Patrick Lumumba
and Tomas Sankara couldn't have done what they did and
couldn't have become the people they became in absence
of the optimistic character. On my side, being a hero
includes being an optimist not a pessimist. For I am an
optimist I feel I will become what I want to become and
fulfill all that I will be required to fulfill.
